what if i would like to upload multiple files?

use a zip archive to combine those files. make sure to only use zip, since other students have to be able to open the archive and zip is probably the most common format.

what if i upload a submission / evaluation again?

your previous upload will be overwritten. feel free to double check this by downloading your uploaded file and comparing it.

what if i have not been able to meet a deadline?

please get in touch with the teaching assistant.

what if the file of a submission i have to evaluate is unknown?

the student that you have to evaluate did not upload a file. he might submit something after the deadline though, so make sure to check back later.
